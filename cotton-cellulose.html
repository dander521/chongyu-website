Cotton Cellulose

  • Nitrocellulose
  • Banknote & Security Paper
  • Other Cellulose Ester & Acetate

       Around the worlds, many countries still use cotton cellulose to make their banknote and security paper. by using three major types of cotton cellulose.

  • Cotton Pulp
  • Cotton Comber Pulp
  • Cotton Linter Pulp

       There are three major grades of Cotton Linter for producing Nitrocellulose:

  • Nitrocellulose Lacquer Grade
  • Nitrocellulose Propellant Grade
  • Nitrocellulose Dynamite Grade

      Cotton linter is used to produce a wide range of products, such as CMC, MC, HPMC, HEC, AC, and viscose fibers, etc. These modified cellulose are used everywhere in our daily life.

There are two major applications for Nitrocellulose. One is lacquer and ink, the other is Propellant and Dynamite
